A short overview of the environment and ecosystems within New Jersey!

Located in the North Eastern part of the United States, New Jersey is well known for its landscapes, diverse ecosystems and incredible wildlife. The state is home, to a range of habitats that support plants and animals spanning from sea level up to an elevation of 1,823 feet (550m). Within the forests of this region you can find bears, white-tailed deer and other fascinating creatures. The captivating marshes also attract migratory birds. Vigorous conservation efforts are consistently being carried out to protect this biodiversity and ensure the well being of New Jerseys ecosystems. With its abundance of nature based activities and a strong commitment to preservation, New Jersey offers a blend where nature seamlessly intertwines with human involvement.
